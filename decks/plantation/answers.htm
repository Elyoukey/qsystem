Why did they die ?
Because they knew something about the foreman


Ernestine had access to the account book and discovered that the foreman was falsifying it to resell some of the plantation production.
She went to ask Siméon if it was possible and they were caught on the field.
The foreman heard them and decided that he had to kill them both.
